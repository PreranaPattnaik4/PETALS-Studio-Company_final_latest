# **App Name**: Petals Studio

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Storybook Gallery: An immersive, masonry-style art gallery featuring watercolor transitions and full-screen lightboxes for premium illustrations.
- Lore Lexicon: A character-driven database managing profiles for figures like Alora and RoseBella, featuring interactive hover-animations and backstory links.
- Dreamweaver Lead Portal: Integrated newsletter and contact submission system that stores user interactions and project inquiries directly in a Firestore database.
- AI Creation Architect: A tool that uses generative AI to provide narrative context and poetic descriptions for 'AI-Assisted' art pieces, bridgeing digital craft and storytelling.
- Dynamic World Hub: A headless CMS-driven blog and storytelling engine powered by Firestore, supporting rich media categorization from world-building to animation updates.
- Magical Theater: A high-performance responsive video player integrated with YouTube for showcasing cinematic shorts and animation previews.
- Crystal Rose Spotlight: A specialized landing interface for the 'Crystal Rose' featured universe, utilizing parallax scrolling and petal-float animations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color is Rose Pink (#F7B7C3), evoking a sense of blooming emotion and softness.
- Background is a creamy Pearl White (#FFF9F5), highly desaturated and bright to suggest an open, clean, watercolor-ready canvas.
- Accent color is Soft Lavender (#DCCFFF), providing an analogous contrast that enhances the mystical and magical feel of the UI.
- Headline font is 'Playfair' (Serif), chosen for its high-contrast elegant look suitable for a premium gallery; Body font is 'PT Sans' (Sans-serif) to ensure modern readability across longer lore segments.
- Three-column cinematic hero structure on the landing page, transitioning into elegant masonry grids for the portfolio to maximize artistic visual impact.
- Micro-interactions including floating petal particles, soft-fade entry transitions, and subtle parallax for the background storybook textures.
- Custom floral-inspired iconography with soft rounded edges and gold-flecked line art to maintain the luxury fantasy brand identity.
